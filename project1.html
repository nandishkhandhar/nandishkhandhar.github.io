Project 1 - Colorizing the Prokudin-Gorskii photo collection

LOW RES IMAGES - Naive search

For the low resolution images, the algorithm used is a simple naive search over the x and y axis. It moves the red and green channels over a range of 10 pixels ([-10, 10]) on both axes and tries to find the optimal allignment by using the L2 norm It then translates the images and stacks the channels to create the final image.
